ABOUT FTIJPI!

A group to share things that you'd rather just print than spend stupid amounts of money on...

Or, for those with humor and whimsy:

"Thou shalt enter this group upon where we rest our visual representations of polymer-printed functional objects,

and thou shalt enjoy and be inspired so, to polymer-print thy own, and share amongst other polymer-printing individuals,

so thy can scream unto the heavens, together, 'F**k that, we shalt simply printeth it!'"

FTIJPI founded by Steven Ryall and Brian Skoog
Website built using Processing 3 & P5.js
